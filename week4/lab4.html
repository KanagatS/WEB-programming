PLACES TO VISIT

DUBAI

Skyscraper Burj Khalifa Dubai is famous primarily for its skyscrapers and futuristic architectural structures. One of the most famous buildings is the Burj Khalifa skyscraper, by the way, the highest in the world (828 meters). From the observation deck on the 124th floor (at a height of 472 meters) offers a magnificent view of the city.




In Dubai mall, the most unusual thing is the oceanarium inside it. More than 33,000 marine animals and fish live here! You can see this beauty by walking along the tunnel, laid through the entire length of the aquarium. The review is fantastic - 270 degrees! And you can take pictures as much as you want.




Even in the prohibitively expensive Dubai, you can find free entertainment. One of the most interesting places that you can see for $0 is the dancing and singing fountain next to the Burj Khalifa skyscraper. Every evening, the jets of the fountain are illuminated by lights, accompanied by the best classical, national and contemporary music.




Wild Wadi Waterpark. The perfect place for thrill seekers. There are as many as 30 non-banal attractions. For the most daring - dizzying slides with the effect of free fall and pools with the effect of a storm. More calm, but no less interesting slides in the water park, of course, also exist.
